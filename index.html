Compete for the spotlight and earn rewards on Blaze!


This is like a reverse hot-potato game where spotlight_bot spotlights a random live stream to earn points over time and from engagement. The spotlight may move to another channel but try to keep it as long as you can. The top scorer each week wins 100 votes and 25% of the BSP.

spotlight_bot is not affiliated with the blaze team. It was created by jt_squared2 as a way to bring more attention and support to channels that may not otherwise get it.

üìñ How It Works

  • A random active stream receives the spotlight
  • If the spotlight finds you randomly keep streaming for 30 minutes or lose it
  • If you gain +10 viewers during your spotlight, it transfers to another random stream
  • After 30 minutes, the spotlight is "unlocked" and can be stolen
  • If stolen there is no 30 minute stream requirement but also no protection from it being stolen again
  • Spotlight status stays with the channel even after the stream stops and points continue to accrue
üíé Stealing the Spotlight (after 30 min):
  • Donate 100 BLAZE tokens to spotlight_bot ‚Üí spotlight moves to a random active stream
  • Subscribe to spotlight_bot ‚Üí spotlight moves to YOUR channel
